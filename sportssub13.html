Oman to host inaugural FIH Hockey Nations Cup 2




Oman is set to make history by hosting the inaugural edition of the FIH Hockey Nations Cup 2, a landmark event in the international hockey calendar. This prestigious tournament will bring together emerging hockey nations from around the world, providing them with a platform to compete at a high level and showcase their growing talent.

The decision to host the tournament in Oman underscores the country's commitment to promoting hockey and fostering a vibrant sports culture. With its modern infrastructure and growing reputation as a host for international events, Oman is poised to deliver an exceptional experience for players, officials, and fans. The tournament will be held at the Sultan Qaboos Sports Complex, a state-of-the-art facility that has previously hosted a range of high-profile sports events.

The FIH Hockey Nations Cup 2 is a vital step in the development of global hockey, offering participating nations the opportunity to gain exposure and experience against competitive teams. The event also serves as a pathway for promotion to higher levels of international competition, making it a critical milestone for teams aiming to establish themselves on the world stage.
